Rhubarb Cake Recipe

A recipe of a classic soft and moist rhubarb cake that is easy to make and tastes absolutely lovely with vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 400 g rhubarb
  • 1/2 dl + 1 dl sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 dl wheat flour
  • 1 tsk baking powder
  • 1 tsk vanilla sugar
  • 1/2 dl milk

Instructions

  1. Turn on the oven on to 175Â°C
  2. Cut the rhubarbs into natural cube-like pieces.
  3. Place the rhubarb pieces in a cake pan and sprinkle 1/2 dl sugar on top of it.
  4. Put the pan with the rhubarbs in the oven and let it soften while you continue in the recipe.
  5. Put the wheat flour, baking powder and vanilla sugar in bowl and use a whisk to blend it evenly together.
  6. Take another bowl where you whisk up 1 egg with 1dl sugar to a fair foam that almost stick to the bowl when you turn it over.
  7. Take a sleave, or preferably a dough scraper and gently lift and mix the flour mix into the foam.
  8. Add 1/2 dl of milk and stear it in gently until mixed in.
  9. Take out the pan whit the rhubarbs from the oven and pour the batter evenly over the rhubarbs in the pan.
  10. Bake in the oven for 30-35 min.
  11. When it is finished and have a lovley golden brown color, take it out and let it cool down a bit under a baking towel.
  12. Serve with vanilla ice cream.
